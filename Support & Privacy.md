This page provides legal information and assistance for the PsyScale application.
1. Privacy Policy

The PsyScale app is designed to strictly respect medical confidentiality and the privacy of both patients and practitioners.

    No Data Collection: The application does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or medical data.

    Local Processing: All score calculations (MADRS, PANSS, HDRS, YMRS, MAS, HAM-A, GAF) are performed exclusively on your device.

    Total Anonymity: No user account is required, and the app does not access your contacts or your location.

3. Medical References

Calculation algorithms are based on standard clinical scales used in psychiatry. For more details, please refer to the "Information" section within the application.
